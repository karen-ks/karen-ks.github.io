About

I recently completed my Postgraduate degree in MSc Artificial Intelligence at Teesside University, and will be graduating in July of this year. I am open to work now - remotely and on-site - within but not limited to the fields of Data Science, Engineering and Machine Learning.


I received my BSc (2018) in Actuarial Science and Risk Management from Queen's University Belfast. During my Bachelors degree, I worked for a Pensions team as a Placement Actuarial Student and also as a Pensions administrator. And after that, I worked as a HR Administrator and Personal Assistant.


Prior to starting my Masters, I was self-taught in Python and Machine Learning by completing MOOCs on Coursera. I was keen to expand on my degree in Actuarial Science by learning to create and implement solutions.


I am highly motivated and have strong analytical skills, goal oriented and work well in a team as well as independently. Some of the hobbies I indulge in during my spare time are cooking, baking, reading and drawing.
